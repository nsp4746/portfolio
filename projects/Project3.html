Overview

Worked with organization in Buffalo, NY to develop and implement a comprehensive cybersecurity awareness training program for employees. Also implemented policies and procedures for them to follow for the foreseeable future. This was through the Google Cybersecurity Clinic program.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenges that were encountered were definitely working with a client in a project of this scope. It was my first time working with a client in this regard and it was challenging to get started. However, once we got started, it was a relatively smooth process.

Key Features

  • Feature 1: Developed a comprehensive cybersecurity awareness training program for employees.
  • Feature 2: Implemented policies and procedures for false positives.

Final Results

The client was extremely satisfied with the results and was able to implement the policies and procedures for their organization.
